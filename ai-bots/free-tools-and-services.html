Why Are We Doing This?

With over three decades of Sales and Marketing experience, we’ve seen companies of all sizes struggle with a common challenge: lack of GTM alignment. The culprit? A broken ICP. Teams chase the wrong accounts, and when deals close, Customer Success is left holding the bag. It’s a surefire way to waste valuable resources and alienate customers.
‍
Teams today are scrambling to AI as the silver bullet for all their GTM woes. Its transformational power is undeniable. AI is great for handling mundane tasks and scaling efforts like cold emails (RIP inbox zero). There’s a real place for AI in GTM, but we’re not fans of using AI just for the sake of it.

We're a different approach. We aren't giving you tools to contact more people. And we aren't charging you thousands a month for a fancy UI on top of a LLM. Instead, we are giving you tools, for free, that can help you refine your GTM strategy for 2025.

We’re condensing playbooks, strategies, and best practices to train our own team of robots that deliver real value. These tools are designed to jump-start your 2025 GTM strategy planning.

Want to take it a step further? Request a free consultation to uncover what makes your best customer tick. You will get an in depth report outlining your customer data review, gap analysis, ICP benchmark, and strategic recommendations.

Here's to a prosperous 2025!
