FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Your Questions, Answered

We’ve answered the most common questions to save you time. Still unsure or have more questions? Contact our sales team today!
What is BoostIdeal and how does it work?

BoostIdeal is a GTM scoring platform that allows you to identify and test customer segments and aligns Marketing, Sales, and Customer Success teams around your Ideal Customer Profile. With a data-driven approach, BoostIdeal helps you prioritize accounts, streamline operations, and drive growth across your entire GTM organization.

What is a Dynamic Account Scoring?

BoostIdeal allows you to build, test, and analyze multiple ICPs, evolving them based on market signals and customer behavior. This ensures your ICP and account scores stay relevant and aligns with your team’s needs, making it easier to target the right customers.

What are market signals?

Market signals are real-time external indicators that reveal opportunities or risks, helping you prioritize and engage effectively. These include hiring trends, funding events, tech adoption, recruiting activity, competitor shifts, social engagement, and more. By leveraging these insights, you can target the right accounts at the right time with precision.

What is the advantage of using BoostIdeal for account scoring?

BoostIdeal scores accounts dynamically using key data points, helping your teams prioritize high-value opportunities. The scoring models evolve with your business, ensuring that your Sales and Marketing efforts remain laser-focused on the best prospects.

How does BoostIdeal help align Marketing, Sales, and Customer Success?

By providing a unified scoring model that all teams use, BoostIdeal ensures that Marketing, Sales, and Customer Success are working toward the same goals. The platform delivers standardized fit and readiness scores, allowing each team to take coordinated actions that support overall business objectives.

How does BoostIdeal help me with Customer Success?

BoostIdeal offers customer tiering and health scoring to identify at-risk customers early. With fit and engagement scores, your teams can implement proactive retention strategies to improve customer satisfaction and reduce churn.

How does BoostIdeal streamline account prioritization and territory planning?

BoostIdeal streamlines account prioritization and territory planning by using dynamic scoring models that assess the fit and readiness of each account. This allows your sales teams to focus on high-potential opportunities while optimizing resource allocation across territories.

How do I import data into BoostIdeal?

BoostIdeal allows you to easily upload CSV files with data from your CRM or other systems. BoostIdeal offers bi-directional integrations with popular CRMs, ensuring your data flows smoothly across systems. These integrations will unlock new use cases and allow you to maximize the value of your existing tools with BoostIdeal’s powerful scoring and insights.

What kind of insights can I expect from BoostIdeal?

BoostIdeal allows you to benchmark and derive insights across your GTM organization. You’ll gain visibility into account prioritization, account strategy, customer health, and emerging opportunities, allowing you to optimize GTM efficiency.
